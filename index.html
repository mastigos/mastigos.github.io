Intro

Welcome to the wonderful world of MUDs!


MUDs, short for Multi User Dungeons, originated all the way back in 1980 with MUD1, and was the first Internet multiplayer roleplaying game. In the 90s, with Internet and computer access becoming more ubiquitous, the genre exploded, with countless games being developed and released. Since then, hundreds of thousands of people have immersed themselves in these stories and games, and while the genre is in decline, it's still going strong with many games having hundreds of players at peak.


So...what actually are MUDs?


At the most basic, they're text-based multiplayer games. Some are hack and slash, some are PvP, and many are roleplaying games, where you actually create your character and play as them, directly immersing yourself in the world. Often, these types are blurred and elements of all of them exist in one game!
